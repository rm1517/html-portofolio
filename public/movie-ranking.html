The Best Movies According to Akash

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time.

Avatar & Avatar 2

Avatar is an American media franchise created by James Cameron,
which consists of a planned series of epic science fiction films produced by
Lightstorm Entertainment and distributed by 20th Century Studios,
as well as associated merchandise, video games and theme park attractions.

Spider-man: No Way Home

Spider-Man seeks the help of Doctor Strange to forget his exposed
secret identity as Peter Parker. However, Strange's spell goes horribly wrong,
leading to unwanted guests entering their universe.

Jawan

A man is driven by a personal vendetta to rectify the wrongs in society,
while keeping a promise made years ago.
He comes up against a monstrous outlaw with no fear,
who has caused extreme suffering to many.
